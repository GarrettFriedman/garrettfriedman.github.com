Garrett Friedman: My Biography

      My name is Garrett Friedman. I am a third year student at Tufts University currently studying Biochemistry, graduating with a bachelor's degree from the school of arts and sciences in May 2014.

      I was born in Boston, Massachusetts, on April 10th 1992, and have since then lived in the Boston area.

      I have worked for 5 years in biology having conducted bronchoalveolar stem cell projects with Harvard Medical School, olfactory progenitor cell research at Tufts Medical School, and immunology research at Harvard Medical School. Upon graduation plan to attend medical school
